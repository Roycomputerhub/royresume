1. Information We Collect

When you use the Roy Computer Hub Smart Resume Builder, all data entered (including names, contact details, education, and photos) stays on your local device. We do not store, collect, or transmit your personal resume data to any external servers.

We use third-party advertising companies, such as Google AdSense, to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites.

  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

3. Data Security

Because the resume generation happens entirely in your web browser (client-side), your data is as secure as your own device. We do not maintain databases of user resumes.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
